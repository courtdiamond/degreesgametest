How to Play

Connect two seemingly unrelated people or things by finding the hidden chain between them.

1 You'll see a start and an end — your job is to connect them.
2 At each step, pick from 4 options. One is the correct link in the chain.
3 Wrong picks are dead ends — real connections, but not on the right path.
4 Use Undo (×2) to forgive a dead end, or Hint (×1) to eliminate a wrong option.
5 4 dead ends and the game is over — the chain is revealed.

Example:

Leonardo DiCaprio → Kate Winslet → Hugh Jackman → Elmo

DiCaprio & Winslet co-starred in Titanic, Winslet & Jackman co-starred together, Jackman appeared on Sesame Street!
